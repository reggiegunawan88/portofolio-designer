'Independence Day' Interactive Game

From last 2 years, enjoyment for independence day are forgotten because of the covid-19 global pandemic. The boisterous fun atmosphere of crowds cheering in the competition are no more. This idea arose to remember that feeling and give encouragement in the midst of pandemic.

Project Duration

14 July 2021 - 13 August 2021

My Role

Solo designer - Discovery, Research, UI/UX Design, Interaction Design
