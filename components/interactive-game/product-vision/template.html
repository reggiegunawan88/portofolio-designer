Product Vision & Solution

Some of the business goals and user needs that we set at the beginning are as follows:

  • Increase engagement with Kompas.id with this interactive game
  • Gain acquisition of new registered users
  • Celebrate and remember the euphoria of 17 August independence day
  • Want users from all age range to be able to play the game easily

I started the design process by conduct competitor analysis and looking for similar independence game and feature to get design inspiration. By that, I gained insights and differ important notes bases on what's good and not suitable feature to our independence game.
